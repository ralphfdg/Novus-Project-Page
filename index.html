B.R.I.M.

Barangay Resident Information and Management System

Digitizing and automating the entire resident registration and service request workflow for faster, smarter local governance.

Our Problem

Traditional, manual registration processes in barangays are often tedious, prone to human error, and inefficient. This results in long queues, missing documents, and delayed public services.

Our Solution

We digitize and automate the entire workflow, creating a centralized, secure, and easily accessible database for local government units, eliminating paper trails and administrative bottlenecks.

Target Users

Barangay Residents: Need an easy way to register, pay fees, and request services online.

Barangay Staff: Require an organized dashboard to verify, approve, and manage resident records efficiently.

System Workflow & Features

1

Resident Accesses Portal

The resident opens the barangay online registration portal and fills out the resident registration form.

2

Form Submission via Zapier

The form is submitted through Zapier, which automatically captures the resident’s information and triggers an automation workflow.

3

API Data Transfer

Zapier sends the submitted form data to the Barangay Resident Management System using an API for automatic processing.

4

System Validation

The system validates the resident’s information to ensure all required details are complete and that there are no duplicate records.

5

Invalid Information Handling

If the information is incomplete or invalid, the system sends an SMS notification to the resident requesting correction.

6

Resident Record Creation

If the information is valid, the system creates a new resident record in the barangay database.

7

Payment Processing

If the requested barangay service requires a fee, the system generates an online payment request and the resident completes the payment through a payment gateway.

8

Payment Confirmation

After payment is completed, the payment gateway sends confirmation to the system via API.

9

SMS Notification

The system sends an SMS notification confirming successful registration or payment to the resident.

10

Staff Verification

Barangay staff review the resident’s submitted information and supporting documents through their organized dashboard.
