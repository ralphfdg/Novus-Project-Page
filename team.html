Meet the Development Team

Kobe James Ferrer

Backend Developer

Kobe specializes in the server-side logic, database architecture, and API integrations that power the core of the registration system. He ensures that data flows securely and efficiently from automated Zapier workflows straight into the barangay's central database. His technical expertise lies in building resilient systems capable of handling complex validations, automated SMS triggers, and payment gateways. Kobe is dedicated to maintaining high performance, security, and data integrity across all back-end operations.
